how 2 use noted

phase 1: logging the album

log a new record using the "add new record" form (add the "record title", "artist/band name", "year the album was released", and any "comments" you have (e.g. if your friend recommended it to you, note it!)

phase 2: adding a liner note

after you've listened to the album you've logged, you can update the album to have a liner note! on the liner note, you'll add your rating of the album, a "standout track" (which track stood out the most to you), and any additional thoughts you want to log about the record.
...and that's all there is to it! as long as you remember your login credentials, you'll find your records all in one place and can add any others along the way!

other features:

★ check out the 'standout mix', where you'll find a playlist of all your standout tracks! the more standout tracks you log, the more you'll see on your mix :^ )
